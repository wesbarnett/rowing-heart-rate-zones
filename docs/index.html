About

The formula used for each zone above is:
(MHR - RHR) * % + RHR

The maximum heart rate (MHR) is calculated using the age input. Every year 30 and below is expected to increase maximum heart rate by the formula 220-age. After age 30 each year counts only half a beat in that difference.

The percentages used are as follows:
  • UT2 = 59%-67%
  • UT1 = 67%-75%
  • AT = 75%-85%
  • TR = 85%-100%
